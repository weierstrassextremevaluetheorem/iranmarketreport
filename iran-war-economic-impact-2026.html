📅 Conflict Timeline
Feb 28, 2026
Operation Epic Fury Launched
Joint US-Israeli airstrikes targeting Iranian leadership and military infrastructure. Supreme Leader Khamenei assassinated in precision decapitation strike. Oil futures spike 10–13% overnight.
Mar 1–2, 2026
Iran Retaliates · Gulf States Hit
Iran launches missiles and drones at US/Israeli targets and Gulf states hosting US forces. Airspace closes across UAE, Qatar, Kuwait. Dow falls 400+ points. Strait of Hormuz effectively shut down.
Mar 3–4, 2026
Asian Markets Crash · Qatar Force Majeure
South Korea KOSPI crashes 12%, triggering circuit breaker — worst since 2008. Thai Stock Exchange curbs trading. Qatar declares force majeure on LNG exports. Ras Tanura refinery closed.
Mar 5–7, 2026
Supply Chain Cascade Begins
~200 ships stranded in Persian Gulf. Air freight costs up 400%. Indian pharma exports disrupted. AWS data center hit by drone shrapnel in Dubai — first cloud infrastructure war damage in history. IEA calls emergency meeting.
Mar 8–9, 2026
Oil Tops $100 · Peak Panic
Brent hits $119.50/barrel intraday — highest in 4 years. WTI at $101.97. US gasoline at $3.45/gal. S&P 500 falls 0.94%. Analysts warn of "game-changing" energy crisis.
Mar 10–11, 2026
Oil Retreats on Trump Comments
Trump states war will end "very soon." WTI falls 11.94% to $83.45; Brent loses 11.28% to $87.80. Markets partially recover. Strait of Hormuz still closed. Structural damage remains unassessed.
